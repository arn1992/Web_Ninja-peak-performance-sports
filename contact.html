Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 business days) is available at checkout. Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all unused items in their original packaging. Simply contact us to initiate a return and we'll guide you through the process.

Are all products authentic?

Yes, 100%. We source all products directly from official brand distributors and authorised wholesalers. Every item comes with the manufacturer's original warranty.

Do you offer size guidance?

Yes! Each product page includes a detailed size guide. Our team is also available to help you find the perfect fit — just send us a message or give us a call.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can monitor your delivery in real time from dispatch to door.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within the United States. International shipping is coming soon — sign up for our newsletter to be notified when we expand to your country.
